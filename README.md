scenario :
	The kingdom is in drought !
	After a wave of repression against the blood cult ordered by the king, the cult leader cursed 
	the kingdom to not have any rains for the next century, this cannot go on any longer, the king
	tasked you, the hero, to find a solution to this predicament !
	So, you head towards the cult's main base to find and kill the cult leader, to free the land
	of this terrible curse !
	Along your way, you will encounter various cult members, monsters, but also allies to help you
	in your quest. Many secrets are hidden in the cultist base's old walls...

////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

commands :
	- HELP :
		shows all available commands
	- HELP <command name> :
		shows the manual entry for that command
	- LOOK :
		shows all the visible content of the location you're currently in
	- LOOK <args> :
		shows the description of the elements in argument
	- GO <location name> :
		try to pass through the exit going towards the location argument
	- USE <things>:
		tries to use what is specified in argument, like opening a door with a key,
		pulling a lever or opening a chest
		tip : try things out !
	- TAKE ALL :
		tries to take all items currently in the room and puts them in your inventory
	- TAKE <item> :
		tries to take the item in argument and put it in your inventory
	- DROP <item> :
		moves the item in argument to the room you're currently in
	- FIGHT <enemy> :
		enters a fight with an enemy
		/!\WARNING/!\
			an enemy may already be in combat, and others can join the fight !
			you may also be forcibly taken into combat by certain encounters
	- QUIT :
		exit the game and saves.
		we hope you had a good experience !
